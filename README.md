# EPI Fire Protection Inc.

> EPI Fire Protection Inc. is a Greater Toronto Area–based fire protection company with over 33 years in business. We specialize in the inspection, testing, and maintenance of fire protection systems — including fire alarm, sprinkler, suppression, emergency lighting, and portable fire extinguisher systems — as well as deficiency repairs and code-compliance work. We serve hospitals, municipalities, school boards, property managers, commercial building owners, and industrial facilities across the GTA and surrounding region.
